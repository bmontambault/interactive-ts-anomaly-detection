Form of Consent

You are invited to participate in a study conducted by the Visual Analytics Lab at Tufts University. Please read the following carefully before deciding to participate. You may participate in this study if you are between the ages of 18 and 65 and are comfortable communicating in English. We expect the entire study to take about 15 minutes maximum, but you will be allotted 60 minutes to complete the study. Your participation in the study is completely voluntary. If you choose to participate in the study you are free to revoke your consent and terminate the study at any time without penalties.
There are no direct benefits or forseeable risks to you for participating in this study. However, if you decide to take part, you will play an integral role in the development of future visualizations. Any information collected in this study will be entirely confidential. The questionnaire that you would be required to complete will only be identifiable by an assigned ID number, and will not be associated with your name or any personally identifiable information.

Purpose of the Study

We aim to better understand how people make judgements based on visualizations. The result of this study would help in the improvement of future visualization designs.

Task Description

You will be shown several visualizations and asked to make judgements based on them. Please try to respond as accurately as possible. Afterwards, you will be asked to complete a demographic questionnaire and provide any additional feedback you wish.
You will be paid a base price of $2.00 and an additional $1.00 if your responses are above an accuracy threshold. Total possible compensation will be $3.00.
